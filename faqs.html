FAQs

Where can I play the developer's games?

You can visit our itch.io or Steam page to play our games.

Are the games developed by just one person?

Most of our games are created by a small core team, but we also collaborate with other developers, composers, and artists depending on the project.

Can I support the development of your games?

Yes! You can support us by wishlisting our games on Steam, purchasing our titles, sharing our work, or joining our community channels.

Do you accept collaborations or commissions?

We’re open to working with partners, publishers, composers, and artists on suitable projects. Feel free to contact us to discuss opportunities.

How often do you release new games or updates?

Release schedules vary by project, but we share regular updates on our social media and development blogs.

What genres of games do you develop?

We create cozy, story-driven, and simulation-style games, but we’re always exploring new ideas and styles.

How can I contact the developer?

You can reach us anytime at yahallogames@gmail.com or through our social media pages.
